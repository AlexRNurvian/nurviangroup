About Nurvian Group

Where science meets strategy.

Nurvian Group is an independent advisory practice providing board-level counsel across strategy, technology, governance, and science — founded and led by Dr Alexander Reip PhD.


Founded on decades of experience at the intersection of deep technology, clean energy, corporate governance, and strategic leadership, Nurvian works with boards, founders, institutions, and investors who need a trusted principal with genuine technical and commercial depth.


Dr Reip brings a career spanning the founding and commercialisation of Oxford nanoSystems Ltd, board and NED roles across clean energy, digital infrastructure and ethics, and senior advisory appointments at leading research universities. Nurvian operates wherever boards need an independent voice that genuinely understands what they are building and why it matters.

Technology & Commercialisation

From bench to boardroom — the full journey.

As Founder, CEO and CTO of Oxford nanoSystems Ltd, Dr Reip invented and commercialised the nanoFLUX® surface coating technology, grew the company from two people to eighteen, achieved profitability, and delivered a successful acquisition. Along the way he secured three ISO certifications, five patents, and Royal Society recognition. This is not theoretical knowledge — it is hard-won experience of what it takes to build and scale a deep technology business from first principles.

Governance & Institutional Leadership

Independent. Rigorous. Trusted at the highest level.

Alongside commercial leadership, Dr Reip has held senior independent roles across public, private, and academic institutions — including NED positions at the Net Zero Technology Centre and BindEthics, the Chair of the Oxfordshire Economic Partnership Board, and Trustee of the Royal Society of Chemistry. He chaired the first industry-led accreditation review for UK chemistry degrees and serves as Honorary Professor and Royal Society Entrepreneur in Residence at Brunel University London.
